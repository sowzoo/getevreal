## Why this matters
The sticker price is only one part of the EV decision. A smarter checklist compares home charging cost, insurance changes, expected road-trip use, and what gear you may need right away.

## What to include
- Monthly payment target.
- Home charging setup cost.
- Insurance quote delta.
- Range needs for a normal week.
- Accessory budget for cables, mats, and adapters.
