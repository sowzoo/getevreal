## The goal
Readers trust notes that sound personal and specific. Document what felt natural, what felt unfamiliar, and what you still need to research.

## Strong sections for this format
- Cabin experience.
- Visibility and comfort.
- Regenerative braking feel.
- Tech features you would actually use.
- Questions that still need answers before buying.
